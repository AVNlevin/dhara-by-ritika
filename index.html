The Designer

The Art of Handcrafted Luxury

At Dhara by Ritika, we believe a gown is more than just clothing; it is a canvas of emotions, dreams, and meticulous artistry. Founded on the principles of traditional haute couture, our atelier designs pieces that embody grace, elegance, and distinct personal styles.

Every drape of silk, touch of lace, and hand-sewn crystal is curated to celebrate the individual. From sketch to final fitting, we pour hours of specialized craftsmanship into designing bridal couture and luxury evening garments that fit perfectly and feel transcendent.
